Help & contact

Every message sent to DeadStick Digital is answered by a human — usually the one who wrote the code. We aim to reply within one business day (Mon–Fri, US Mountain Time).

Fastest path: send us a note from the contact form on the homepage. Include your device (e.g. “iPhone 15, iOS 18.2”), the app and version, and a short description of what went wrong. Screenshots can be sent once we reply.

Frequently asked questions

How do I request a refund?

Refunds for App Store and Google Play purchases are handled by the respective platform. On Apple, visit reportaproblem.apple.com. On Google Play, open the Play Store app → Menu → Account → Purchase history. If the platform denies a request you believe is reasonable, reach us through the contact form and we will help.

Do you collect my data?

No. See our Privacy Policy for the full details. The short version: we do not operate analytics or advertising SDKs, we do not maintain user accounts, and our apps keep your data on your own devices by default.

How do I delete my data?

Because none of our apps create a user account on our servers, there is nothing on our side to delete. Our data deletion page walks through, per app, how to remove the local database, clear keychain entries, and (if you enabled sync) delete the app's container from your iCloud or Google Drive. If you would like written confirmation that we hold no copy of your data, send us a Privacy request through the contact form and we will reply within one business day.

I reported a bug. When will it be fixed?

If you've reported a bug, we'll acknowledge it within one business day and, once we've reproduced it, we'll give you an estimate. Most non-blocking bugs ship in the next point release; security-relevant issues are patched faster.

Will my purchase carry forward to future versions?

Yes. When we release a major update to an app you have already paid for, we do not force you to pay again for functionality you already owned. If we ever introduce a new tier with new features, the features you previously had remain available to you.

How can I report a security issue?

If you believe you have found a vulnerability, please report it privately through our contact form (select Privacy request or Something else, and mark the topic as security in the first line of your message). Do not disclose the issue publicly until we have had a reasonable opportunity to respond. We will acknowledge your report within one business day.
