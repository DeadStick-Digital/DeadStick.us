Frequently asked questions

How do I request a refund?

Refunds for App Store and Google Play purchases are handled by the respective platform. On Apple, visit reportaproblem.apple.com. On Google Play, open the Play Store app → Menu → Account → Purchase history. If the platform denies a request you believe is reasonable, write to contact@deadstick.us and we will help.

Do you collect my data?

No. See our Privacy Policy for the full details. The short version: we do not operate analytics or advertising SDKs, we do not maintain user accounts, and our apps keep your data on your own devices by default.

I reported a bug. When will it be fixed?

If you've reported a bug by email, we'll acknowledge it within one business day and, once we've reproduced it, we'll give you an estimate. Most non-blocking bugs ship in the next point release; security-relevant issues are patched faster.

Will my purchase carry forward to future versions?

Yes. When we release a major update to an app you have already paid for, we do not force you to pay again for functionality you already owned. If we ever introduce a new tier with new features, the features you previously had remain available to you.

How can I report a security issue?

If you believe you have found a vulnerability, please report it privately to contact@deadstick.us. Do not disclose the issue publicly until we have had a reasonable opportunity to respond. We will acknowledge your report within one business day.
