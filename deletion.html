Account & data deletion

DeadStick Digital does not operate user accounts. There is nothing stored on our servers to delete — the only copies of your data live on your own devices and, if you opted in, in your own cloud account. This page explains how to remove each copy.

Short version: uninstall the app to wipe its local database. If you turned on iCloud or Google Drive sync, delete the app's container/folder in that service to remove the remote copy. We never receive either copy. No message to us is required.

ClawMelt

ClawMelt stores every dataset, checkpoint, adapter, and log file in a local project folder on your Mac. Nothing is uploaded to DeadStick Digital or to any third party during training.

  • Remove a single project. In ClawMelt, right-click the project in the sidebar and choose Delete, or delete the project folder directly in Finder.
  • Remove everything. Drag ClawMelt.app to the Trash, then delete ~/Library/Application Support/ClawMelt/ and ~/Library/Caches/com.deadstick.clawmelt/. That removes preferences, the model catalog cache, and any local training logs.
  • Hugging Face-side data. Models or datasets you downloaded from Hugging Face live on your machine only. They are not mirrored anywhere by DeadStick Digital.

BillingBird

BillingBird stores clients, invoices, receipts, and attachments in an on-device database. Sync, if enabled, writes an encrypted container to your own iCloud or Google Drive account. DeadStick Digital cannot read either copy.

  • On-device. Uninstalling BillingBird from your phone removes the local database. iOS: long-press the app → Remove App → Delete App. Android: long-press → App info → Uninstall.
  • iCloud sync copy. Open Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → BillingBird → Delete Data. This removes the encrypted BillingBird container from your iCloud.
  • Google Drive sync copy. Open drive.google.com → Settings (gear) → Manage apps → find BillingBird → Options → Delete hidden app data.
  • Export first, if you want. Before deleting, you can export your full records as a .billingbird archive from Settings → Export.

CarrierPigeon

CarrierPigeon does not store your traffic, destinations, or metadata anywhere — not on your devices, not on ours. The only local state is pairing configuration: WireGuard keys and the identifier of the phone you paired with.

  • Clear pairing. Open the desktop or phone companion → Settings → Clear pairing. This deletes the WireGuard keypair and paired device identifier from the system keychain on both devices.
  • Remove the apps. Uninstalling CarrierPigeon from either device wipes all keychain entries associated with that install.

Our website

This site serves only static pages and does not create cookies, analytics profiles, or accounts. When you submit the contact form on the homepage, your name, email, selected topic, and message are delivered to us over TLS via our form provider (Web3Forms), stored briefly for transit, and then read only by a person at DeadStick Digital. To delete your message from our inbox after we have replied, send us a note asking us to do so and we will remove the thread.

Where to send a deletion request

If you cannot complete one of the steps above, or you would like written confirmation that no copy of your data remains with us, send a note through the contact form with the topic Privacy request. We will acknowledge within one business day and respond in writing. Because we hold no user data at rest, most requests are answered with a short explanation and a pointer to the steps above.
