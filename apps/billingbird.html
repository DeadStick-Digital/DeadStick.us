BillingBird

Invoicing, receipts, and client bookkeeping that fits in your pocket. Scan a receipt, send a professional invoice, and keep clean books — without a monthly subscription to a platform that owns your records.

Contact support → Learn more

Overview

BillingBird is a mobile-first invoicing app for freelancers, contractors, and small service businesses. It handles clients, invoices, receipts, payments, templates, and expense reports — the whole paper trail — in one app, on your phone.

What it does

  • Create and send professional PDF invoices in seconds, with line items, categories, and tax rules.
  • Scan paper receipts with your camera and attach them to a client or expense report.
  • Track payments, partial payments, and outstanding balances.
  • Maintain a clean client directory with notes, default rates, and contact history.
  • Export a full .billingbird archive anytime — your data is never stuck.
  • Choose between six color schemes (ocean, forest, sunset, berry, slate, ember) with full light and dark modes.

How your data is stored

BillingBird stores everything in a local on-device database. If you want your records on more than one device, you can enable optional sync via Apple iCloud or Google Drive. In that case your data is encrypted in transit and kept in your own cloud account — DeadStick Digital does not operate a server that can read your BillingBird data. See the privacy policy for details.

Platform

Shipping: iOS 17 or later on iPhone and iPad; Android 12 or later.
Framework: React Native 0.76 (New Architecture) with Expo SDK 52.

Accessibility

BillingBird supports VoiceOver on iOS and TalkBack on Android, respects Dynamic Type, and ships with a high-contrast palette for users with low vision. If you find an accessibility issue, please send us a note through the contact form.
