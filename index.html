The Studio

What we believe.

DeadStick Digital is a boutique software studio. We sign our own releases, answer our own email, and write our own code. These are the principles we try to keep.

01

Your device, your data.

Every app ships with a local-first architecture. Sync is optional, encrypted, and routed through services you already trust — iCloud, Google Drive — not our servers.

02

No tracking. No ads. Your data is yours.

We don't collect analytics about how you use our apps, and don't embed third-party programs to sell your data. We love our privacy, and we know you love yours, too.

03

Quiet, honest releases.

We ship when the build is right — not when marketing calls. Every feature is reviewed, every regression is owned, and every change is sincere and with purpose. For your betterment.
