The Studio

What we believe.

DeadStick Digital is a boutique software studio. We sign our own releases, answer our own email, and write our own code. These are the principles we try to keep.

01

Your device, your data.

Every app ships with a local-first architecture. Sync is optional, encrypted, and routed through services you already trust — iCloud, Google Drive — not our servers.

02

No tracking. No ads.

We don't collect analytics about how you use our apps, don't embed third-party SDKs, and don't build an advertising business. You pay us once — or not at all. That's the deal.

03

Quiet, honest releases.

We ship when the build is right — not when marketing calls. Every feature is reviewed, every regression is owned, and every change is explained in a human-readable changelog.
